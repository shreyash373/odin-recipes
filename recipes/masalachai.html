Masala Chai

Description

This is a Indian beverage made by brewing black tea with a blend of aromatic spices like cardamom, ginger, cinnamon, and cloves, then typically sweetened and combined with milk

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons loose black tea (or 2 tea bags)
  • 2-3 teaspoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 2-3 green cardamom pods, lightly crushed
  • 1 small piece cinnamon stick
  • 3-4 whole cloves
  • 1 small piece fresh ginger, grated or sliced
  • 2-3 black peppercorns (optional)

Steps

  1. Pour water into a saucepan and bring it to a boil.
  2. Add crushed cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, ginger, and peppercorns to the boiling water.
  3. Let the spices simmer for 2-3 minutes to release their flavors.
  4. Add loose tea leaves (or tea bags) and simmer for another 2 minutes.
  5. Pour in milk and sugar, then stir well.
  6. Let the chai simmer for 3-4 minutes until it reaches a rich color and aroma.
  7. Strain the chai into cups using a tea strainer.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy.
