MAC'N'CHEESE

Description

This is a simple comfy food which tastes super good

Ingredients

  • Macaroni Pasta
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in water for 10 min or until you feel its cooked and add salt to the water
  2. Take a pot and pour milk and keep it in low heat and add the cooked pasta after 2 min
  3. Stir the pot for 5-7 minutes then add Cheese(grate it and add)
  4. add some butter and stir until you can feel the creamy texture
  5. pour it into a plate and enjoy your creamy fresh Mac'N'Cheese
